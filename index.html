About us

In 2009, Pat and Sarah Schloss moved to Webster Township, Michigan nestled between Dexter and Ann Arbor with four kids and no experience raising livestock. The number of children and our love for the ever growing number of sheep, cows, pigs, chickens, and bees continues to grow. We have become quite proud of our ability to provide our family and people in our community with an all-natural source of meat from animals raised the way you would expect them to be raised. The responsibility and comroderie of raising animals has also proven to be an excellent approach to nurturing our children to become responsible and compassionate adults. Be sure to check out our Farm Diary to learn the latest news at Eight Kids Farm.

What we offer

Our farm specializes in producing meat, eggs, honey, and wool from a diverse collection of animals that are raised without the use of anitibiotics or artificial hormones. Be sure to check out our animals and husbandry practices to learn more about the breeds of animals and how we raise them. Although we do sell some meat by the cut, we commonly sell whole, half, and quarter animals. Many are overwhelmed by receiving cuts of meat that they've never cooked. Be sure to check out our collection of family favorite recipes. The kids also love making wool products that are always for sale and can be made to order. More than anything, we hope to offer members of our community an experience that improves their well-being.
