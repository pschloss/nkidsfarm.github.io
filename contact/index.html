Contact information

Have a question? Part of our mission is to help educate people about animal farming and perhaps even encourage them to start their own farm. Feel free to get in touch with us online via facebook, Instagram, twitter, email, or phone.

Want to buy something? You can send us money via PayPal. Talk with us or send us an email first so we know what you are paying for and have the right amount.

Want to visit or send your pigeon? We love having visitors and kids especially enjoy meeting all of the various animals. But be careful because Papa might put you to work! With the farm and kids we are pretty busy, so send us an email or call before you come. Here's the google-eyed view of Nine Kids Farm

Like this website design? Thanks! You are welcome to download a copy of the website to customize for your own purposes from it's GitHub repository. If you find it useful, please drop us a line and let us know. If you're interested in having a website developed, let us know and we'll see what we can work out.
